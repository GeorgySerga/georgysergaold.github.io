About the idea behind this blog

The main purpose of this minimal blog is to help me express myself. I wanted to develop my home page with relatively new technologies for me, like Harp, Jade, Sass. I wanted something mine, not Blogger or Wordpress. As a side effect I chose font icons for the social networks I use (they are on the left) and chose a new style for SublimeText (old one wasn't so inspiring). Feel free to click on my photo to go to the main page (I'm sorry if it's still empty).

Have a nice day! :)
